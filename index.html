What is Doodle Empires?

Doodle Empires is an innovative strategy game that combines creative map-drawing mechanics with deep historical simulation. In this unique sandbox experience, players can literally draw their own nations on the map, establish borders, and watch as their civilizations evolve through time. Unlike traditional strategy games, Doodle Empires empowers players with complete creative freedom in shaping their historical narratives.

Gameplay Questions

How does the card system work in Doodle Empires?

The card system is the core mechanic of Doodle Empires, featuring different types of cards:

  • EXPAND cards: Allow nations to grow with varying sizes (small, medium, large)
  • SPLIT cards: Create new nations from existing territories
  • UNION cards: Enable nations to merge and form larger empires
  • NEW NATION cards: Start fresh nations in unclaimed territories

What are the different map modes available?

Doodle Empires offers two primary map modes:

  • Political Mode: Shows current borders and territorial control
  • Origin Mode: Displays the cultural and historical origins of nations

Game Features

How do leaders and traits work in Doodle Empires?

Each nation has a randomized leader with unique traits such as Conqueror, Settler, Coward, or Average. These traits influence your nation's expansion capabilities and strategic options. You can use the New Leader card to change leaders when needed.
